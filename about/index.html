About Us

RUSH is a video project launched in October 2023 by a group of multidisciplinary creatives in Berlin to promote electronic music. The aim of the project is to film DJ sets from mostly underrepresented artists in Berlin and beyond with a focus on FLINTA* and BIPoC folks.


We have sessions every first Thursday of the month at the collective-run Caf√© Kukumu in Moabit, Berlin. The sessions are open to everyone who wants to come and hang out with us.


We care about building a community and giving visibility and access to underrepresented artists, creating a safe space for artistic expression, networking and enjoyment around our biggest passion. Our values are grounded in inclusivity, respect, non-discrimination, harmony, solidarity and fellowship.
